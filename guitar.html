What you'll need

Super Glue, 30s works best

Patience

1
Glue the housing with cutout to main body

Using a small amount of super glue (two small dots is enough) glue the body with circle to the main body.
Make sure it is lined up, then wait for it to set.

2
Connect the outer to the guitar

Connect both halves of the outer together, then start at the top and work your way round both sides, one at a time.
Take your time to avoid breakage.

3
Connect closed side to the guitar

Start one side of the outer and make your way round.
A small amount of force is required to make it click on.

4
Make back support

Place the guitar head piece with slots on the bench.
Connect both smaller support pieces in.
Connect the rectangle piece with slots to complete the support.
Glue is not required for this step.

5
Connect the back support to the guitar

Using a small amount of glue at the top, slot the backing into place.
A small amount of downward force is required to keep it lined up.

6
Attach the front head piece to the guitar

Using a small amount of glue at the top and bottom, line the piece up.
Hold together until the glue is dry.

Your guitar is now complete!
